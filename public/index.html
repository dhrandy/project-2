LEARN ABOUT NOPE

Nope is a creative application that saves the most valuable of resources... Time. Nope was created to save you time in lines by displaying the wait time for the restaurant of your choosing around you.

Along with wait times, we conveniently added a minimalistic, yet helpful rating system for: Food, Drinks, Atmosphere, Staff, Cleanliness, and Parking. The system makes it easy to find the highest ratings in the categories you find most important.
